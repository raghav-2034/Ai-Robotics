# Image Prompts & Visual Illustration Guidelines

To maintain a consistent educational brand identity across all chapters and textbooks, please use the following prompts and styling rules when generating graphics.

---

## 1. Visual Style Identity Rules
*   **Art Style**: 3D claymation, soft plastic texture, miniature toy style, cute and rounded.
*   **Aesthetic Colors**: Bright pastel palette (teal accents, warm yellows, soft blues, clean whites).
*   **Backgrounds**: Clean, solid isometric grids or minimal environments (avoid visual clutter).
*   **Mascot Model**: Miko the Robot — a friendly white sphere with blue eyes, cute rubber wheels, and a small antennae.

---

## 2. Dynamic Image Generation Prompts

### Cover Prompt Blueprint
> **Prompt**: Cute 3D character illustration of a friendly round robot with large glowing blue eyes on a clean pastel blue background, soft shadows, clay render style, minimalist layout, isometric perspective, high resolution --ar 4:3 --stylize 250

### Diagram Illustration Prompt Blueprint
> **Prompt**: Claymation isometric diagram showing a cute toy robot next to a computer brain chip, sensors and motors connected with glowing yellow lines, soft plastic textures, warm studio light, white background --ar 4:3 --style raw

### Activity Graphic Prompt Blueprint
> **Prompt**: Close-up of a child's hands assembling a small toy car using bright plastic gears, cute clay style, soft warm colors, clean white background, educational visual aid --ar 16:9 --v 6.0
